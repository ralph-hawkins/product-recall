Important recall information

Whirlpool AKM274/IX Gas Hob

Whirlpool stainless steel, built-in gas hobs with the model number AKM 274/IX, manufactured between September 2014 and March 2016.

In very rare usage conditions the front-left hob leaks carbon monoxide which can be a low risk of adverse health effects through inhalation.

If you have an affected appliance, Whirlpool advises not to use the burner in the front-left corner until it has been repaired by a Whirlpool engineer.

Contact Whirlpool to arrange an visit from an engineer.
